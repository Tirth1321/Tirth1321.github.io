Tips for Calligraphy

Posted on 20 May 2020

There are distinctive parts of the nib and pen and you should know them. Before you even begin stroking, you need know what what you’re dealing with. The actual tool you’ll be using is two parts — the pen holder and the nib. You can purchase a variety of both, but for a beginner, a basic pen holder (either oblique, which is bend, or straight) and a basic nib are fine.
